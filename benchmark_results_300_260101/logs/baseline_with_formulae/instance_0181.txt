\[ \text{QTc} = \frac{\text{QT}}{\sqrt{\text{RR}}} \]

- RR is the RR interval in seconds, calculated as \( \frac{60}{\text{HR}} \) (HR = 158 bpm)

   \[ \sqrt{\text{RR}} = \sqrt{0.3797} \approx 0.6162 \]

3. Divide the QT interval by the square root of RR:
   \[ \text{QTc} = \frac{0.402}{0.6162} \approx 0.6523 \text{ seconds} \]

4. Convert QTc back to milliseconds:
   \[ \text{QTc} = 0.6523 \times 1000 \approx 652.3 \text{ msec} \]
